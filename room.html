Rooms

Backpackers Hostel — Ximen focuses on providing the highest quality accomodation for backpackers. Independent shower facility, selected beds and beddings, personal safe and power sockets are provided for traveler's needs. The room types includes twin, quad bunk beds and single bunk beds for 4 guests. Travelling with a group of friends makes it a fine choice. Meeting other travelers from abroad makes it an even more memorable experience.
