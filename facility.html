Facility

Common Room

One of the greatest features of youth hostel is the common room. Where travelers may interact with one another, the common room is located on the underground level. Computer, TV, and couch are provided for guest to rest or enjoy other's company.

Self Serve Laundry

Self serve laundry is provided with washing and drying machines. One of the necessary facilities for Backpackers guests.

Toiletries

Shampoo and shower gel are provided in the rooms. Other toiletries like toothbrush and towels are available for purchase on the first floor.
