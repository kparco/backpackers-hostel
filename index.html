Backpackers Hostel — Ximen focuses on providing the highest quality accomodation for backpackers.

Independent shower facility, selected beds & beddings, personal safe and power sockets are provided for traveler's needs. The room type includes twin, quad bunk beds, and single bunk beds for 4 guests. Travelling with a group of friends makes it a fine choice. Meeting other travelers from abroad makes it an even more memorable experience.

Service and Facility

Common Room

One of the greatest features of youth hostel is the common room. Where travelers may interact with one another, the common room is located on the underground level. Computer, TV, and couch are provided for guest to rest or enjoy other's company.

Self Serve Laundry

Self serve laundry is provided with washing machine and drying machines. One of the necessary facilities for backpacker guests.

Toiletries

Shampoo and shower gels are provided in the rooms. Other toiletries like tooth brush and towels are available for purchase on the first floor.

About Us

Backpackers Hostel — Ximen is situated near the busy Ximending Shopping Area. We are a youth hostel designed for both local and foreign backpackers. The interior design combines modern art and local culture. The murals are painted by renowned artist Candy Bird including the large pieces in the lobby, common room, and the hallways. It brings out an artistic, young and energetic atmosphere.

The common room located on the underground level provides TV, computer, and comfortable couch for guests to meet friends and relax. The hostel is clean and comfortable, where you may experience the warm and hospitable service in the most budget friendly rates. We welcome you to Backpackers Hostel — Ximen and share your journey.
